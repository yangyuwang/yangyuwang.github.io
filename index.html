I am a second-year master's student in the Computational Social Science program at the University of Chicago, concentrating in sociology. My research attempts to explore how visual contents encode social inequalities, how belief systems shape cultural production, and how these representations, in turn, influence audience attitudes. More broadly, I am passionate about using mathematical and computational methods to measure culture and to uncover the social dynamics embedded in multimodal materials.
